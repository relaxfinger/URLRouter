# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include respectful communication, constructive feedback, and accepting responsibility for mistakes. Harassment, insulting comments, intimidation, and publishing private information without permission are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainer through the repository owner's GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially. Maintainers may remove, reject, or ban contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
